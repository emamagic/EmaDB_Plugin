Entity Annotation Inspection

Description

The Entity Annotation Inspection validates that classes marked with the @Entity annotation meet certain criteria, ensuring consistency in your projectâ€™s entity configuration.

Validation Rules

This inspection verifies the following:

  • @Entity must be applied only to classes, not interfaces or enums.
  • Classes annotated with @Entity must have a public no-argument constructor.

Usage

This inspection helps maintain correct configuration and structure of your entity classes, allowing for better reliability and easier management of entities in your application.
